Background

I have been playing video games since as early I can remember. I like playing RPG games such as The Witcher 3 and Grand Theft Auto and also enjoy playing MMORPGs as well such as old shool classics like Runescape and Ragnarok Online.

My current game

The game that I am playing atm is Hollow Knight. It is a metroidvania like game and I am having a blast exploring and enjoying the gameplay. The map design is incredible as well as the soundtrack. I easily get immersed into the story of the game and time just flies by quickly. I would highly recommend this game to anyone who loves to play platformers or even those who arn't familliar with the genre are welcome to try as I believe the story is compelling enough and the adventure aspect of the game is great!
